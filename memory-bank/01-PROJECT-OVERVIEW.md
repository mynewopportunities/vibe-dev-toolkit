# AutoMSP Project Overview

## Project Name
AutoMSP: AI-Powered Ticket Automation for ServiceNow-Based MSPs

## Executive Summary
AutoMSP is an AI-native solution that automates 40-60% of MSP ticket processing in ServiceNow through intelligent classification, prioritization, and routing. The solution is built specifically for Managed Service Providers managing 50-500 clients.

## Problem Statement
- Average MSP owner spends 15+ hours/week on manual ticket triage
- Current approach results in 2-3 day average response times
- Manual escalations and routing inconsistencies
- Team burnout from repetitive, low-value work
- Growing customer churn due to slow response times

## Solution Value Proposition
- Reduce manual ticket work by 60-70%
- Improve MTTR by 30-40%
- Increase SLA compliance to 95%+
- Free up team capacity for high-value problem-solving
- Empower teams and improve morale

## Target Market
- Managed Service Providers (MSPs)
- Company size: 50-500 managed clients
- Annual revenue: $2-10M+
- Location: North America primarily, expanding globally

## Revenue Model
- SaaS pricing: $500-2,000/month based on client count
- Implementation services: $5,000-15,000 per deployment
- Premium support tier: $3,000-8,000/month
- Target Year 1 ARR: $1M (20 customers @ $50K average)

## Key Success Metrics
- Month 3: 1-2 paying customers, 100+ waitlist
- Month 6: 5+ customers, $15-20K MRR
- Month 9: 10+ customers, $30-35K MRR
- Month 12: 20+ customers, $50-65K MRR ($1M ARR)
