# AutoMSP Business Model

## Business Model Canvas

### Value Proposition
AutoMSP delivers:
- 60-70% reduction in manual ticket triage work
- 30-40% faster ticket resolution time (MTTR improvement)
- Intelligent, AI-powered ticket classification and routing
- No coding required - one-click ServiceNow integration
- Real-time analytics and ROI dashboard
- Human-in-loop safety and control

### Customer Segments
**Primary:** Mid-market MSPs
- 50-500 managed clients
- $2-10M+ annual revenue
- ServiceNow implementations
- Manual or inconsistent ticket processes

**Secondary:** Enterprise MSPs
- 500+ managed clients
- Looking for premium support and white-label options

### Revenue Streams

**1. Subscription SaaS (Primary - 80% revenue)**
- Starter: $499/month (up to 100 clients)
- Professional: $999/month (up to 300 clients)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing (300+ clients)
- Expansion: Average customer LTV $300K+

**2. Implementation Services (Secondary - 10% revenue)**
- Standard deployment: $5,000
- Custom workflows: $5,000-10,000
- Premium onboarding: $3,000-5,000

**3. Premium Support & Consulting (Secondary - 10% revenue)**
- Premium support tier: $3,000-5,000/month
- Strategic consulting: $10,000-50,000 projects
- Custom integrations: $5,000-15,000 each

### Cost Structure

**Fixed Costs**
- Founder salary: $6,000/month
- Cloud infrastructure: $2,000-3,000/month
- Sales & marketing: $3,000-5,000/month
- Operations: $2,000/month
- **Total**: $13,000-16,000/month (ramp to $50K+ by Year 2)

**Variable Costs**
- AI API costs: $0.10-0.15 per ticket
- Support contractor: $1,000-2,000/month per customer
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Customer success: Baked into support

### Key Partnerships
1. **ServiceNow Partners** - Channel partnerships, white-label options
2. **MSP Software Vendors** - ConnectWise, Kaseya, Autotask integrations
3. **MSP Communities** - MSP Camps, Service Leadership Forum
4. **AI Providers** - OpenAI, Anthropic, local model providers

### Key Resources
1. **Technology**: AI models, ServiceNow integration platform
2. **Team**: Full-stack developer, founder/CEO, marketer
3. **Community**: MSP founder network, early adopters
4. **Intellectual Property**: Proprietary MSP workflow templates

### Channels
1. **Community (80% of new customer acquisition)**
   - MSP Camps sponsorship
   - Reddit r/msp engagement
   - Discord communities
   - Service Leadership Forum

2. **Direct Sales (15% of new customer acquisition)**
   - LinkedIn outreach
   - Cold email campaigns
   - Webinars and demos

3. **Partnerships (5% of new customer acquisition)**
   - ServiceNow partner referrals
   - MSP software vendor partnerships
   - Reseller channels

## Financial Projections

### Year 1 (Months 1-12)
- Month 3: 2 customers, $1K MRR
- Month 6: 5 customers, $18K MRR
- Month 9: 10 customers, $35K MRR
- Month 12: 20 customers, $60K MRR ($1M ARR)
- Burn: $180K total
- Break-even: Month 7

### Year 2 (Projected)
- 60+ customers
- $250-300K MRR ($3-3.6M ARR)
- Profitable by month 12
- Team size: 8-10 people

### Unit Economics (Per Customer)
- Average contract value: $50K/year
- Customer acquisition cost: $5-8K
- Customer lifetime value: $300K+
- LTV:CAC ratio: 40:1-60:1
- Payback period: 1.5-2 months

## Funding Strategy
- Bootstrap: $120K founder savings
-  Profitability: Break-even Month 7-8
- Series A: $2-5M by Month 18 (optional growth acceleration)
