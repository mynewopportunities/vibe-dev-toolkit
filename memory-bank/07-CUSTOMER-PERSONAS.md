# AutoMSP Customer Personas

## Primary Persona: Sarah (MSP Owner)
- 45 years old, 15+ years MSP experience
- 150-300 managed clients, $3-5M revenue
- Pain: 300+ tickets/month, 2-3 day response time
- Budget: $1,500-2,500/month
- Goal: Reduce response times, improve team satisfaction

## Secondary Persona: Marcus (Operations Manager)
- 32 years old, automation enthusiast
- Reports to MSP owner
- Pain: Repetitive escalations, inconsistent routing
- Budget: $500-1,000/month
- Goal: Technical capability, operational efficiency

## Tertiary Persona: Jennifer (MSP CFO)
- 48 years old, financial background
- Pain: Labor costs, scaling challenges
- Budget: $5,000-20,000/year
- Goal: ROI, margin improvement
